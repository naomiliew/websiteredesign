Welcome

Welcome to Henry’s Woodworking! We specialize in wood working, focusing on materials such as reclaimed wood, and fashioning into a unique piece. On our webiste you will find custom pieces that we have created for our clients, ranging from bed frames to vanities, and even pub tables. These photos are for your reference so you can see examples of our work, however, feel free to contact us for a quote or with your own ideas, and we’ll do our best to assist you.
